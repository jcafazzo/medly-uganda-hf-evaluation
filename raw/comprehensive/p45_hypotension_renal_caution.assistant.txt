Both UCG and the GDMT tables flag SBP <90 mmHg/symptomatic hypotension and worsening renal function as situations requiring caution/holding rather than escalation: UCG says use ACE inhibitors and beta blockers with caution if SBP <90 and monitor renal function [UCG 4.1.3], and the GDMT tables specify SBP >90 mmHg for enalapril/carvedilol titration and to hold enalapril for symptomatic hypotension or Cr ↑ >30% / K ≥5.5 [GDMT Tables].
