If she can measure it: SpO₂ should be corrected to >95% with oxygen in pulmonary oedema management (so low SpO₂ supports urgency) [UCG 4.1.4].
If she has home BP: use extra caution with ACEi/beta blocker when SBP <90 mmHg [UCG 4.1.3]—and hypotension would further strengthen the need for emergency evaluation.
