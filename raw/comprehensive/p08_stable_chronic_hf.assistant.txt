What each medicine is for (plain language)
Furosemide (Lasix) – “water pill”: helps the kidneys remove extra water and salt to reduce swelling and breathing difficulty; she should expect periodic blood tests [PocketDoktor].
Enalapril (ACE inhibitor): relaxes blood vessels and reduces salt/water retention so the heart pumps more easily; can cause low BP symptoms and sometimes a dry cough [PocketDoktor].

Spironolactone: helps protect the heart from harmful hormones that cause stiffness/scarring; she will need ongoing blood checks for kidney function and potassium [PocketDoktor].
