With SBP 88/56 + dizziness + cool peripheries and a creatinine rise, it is not safe to up‑titrate enalapril or carvedilol right now. UCG explicitly cautions that ACE inhibitors and beta blockers should be used with caution if SBP <90 mmHg and renal function must be monitored [UCG 4.1.3]. The GDMT tables also require SBP >90 mmHg for ACEi and carvedilol titration and list symptomatic hypotension and Cr rise >30% as hold criteria for ACEi [GDMT Tables].
