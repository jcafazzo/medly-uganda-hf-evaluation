Beta blocker (carvedilol): blocks adrenaline so the heart doesn’t work as hard or beat as fast; do not stop suddenly unless told by a doctor [PocketDoktor]. UCG says add beta blocker when stable, increasing gradually [UCG 4.1.3].
Spironolactone: blocks hormones that stiffen/scar the heart; emphasize that he will need blood tests for kidney function and potassium while on it [PocketDoktor]. UCG lists spironolactone as an additional medicine in HF [UCG 4.1.3].
