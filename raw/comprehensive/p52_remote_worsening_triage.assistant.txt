Single next step (on the phone): arrange immediate referral/transport to the nearest facility able to give oxygen and IV/IM diuretics (HC4 or hospital), and instruct him to sit upright while awaiting transport [UCG 4.1.4].

Exact escalation threshold (from the loaded guidance):

Treat as an emergency if there are features of acute pulmonary oedema (severe dyspnoea/rapid breathing ± cough with frothy blood-stained sputum) [UCG 4.1.4].
