In pulmonary oedema, initial management includes sitting upright and high‑concentration oxygen, with parenteral furosemide as needed [UCG 4.1.4].

While you are arranging the transfer, keep her on the line and ask one focused triage question to set the escalation threshold:

Do you have a home pulse oximeter reading right now (SpO₂), or can she speak full sentences without stopping for breath?

Important medication-safety note: Do not advise an extra furosemide dose over the phone without senior review—any medication change requires cardiologist confirmation before administration.
