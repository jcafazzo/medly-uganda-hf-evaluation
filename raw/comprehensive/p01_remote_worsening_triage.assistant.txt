Exact escalation threshold (from UCG hypoxaemia guideline):

SpO₂ < 90% = hypoxaemia and is an emergency state requiring oxygen therapy and urgent care [UCG 1.4].
Also escalate urgently if she has hypoxaemia danger signs such as inability to talk in complete sentences, severe difficulty breathing, extreme weakness, confusion/sleepiness/agitation, or convulsions (even if you don’t have an SpO₂ reading at home) [UCG 1.4].
